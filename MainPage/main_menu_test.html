Welcome to Letter Soup!

You are given 8 letters of the latin
alphabet randomly selected (we
don't cheat, no worries).

You will have to think of a word that
is a valid one (exists in the dict-
ionary of the English Language).

Each of the letters you are going to
place contain score. The score you
get after placing a word is the all
the scores of each letter added up.

If you're the first player, you can
place any word you want.
Otherwise, your word must overlap
with at least one of the letters
placed on the board and every
word when you read left-right and
top-down should be a valid word.

You win, as you might have
guessed by now, if your score is
bigger! So be careful about how
you construct the words!

We have our trade possibility if you
get stuck! You can replace one, two
or three letters if you want.

Your first trade is free (we are very
generous) but every other will cost
you 5 points (again, we are very
generous)!

Don't rush with your decisions!

1. Place words such that contain
letters not that frequently used.
This way, you reduce the chance
your opponent to get some
common letters already on the
board for him.

2. Avoid placing word that overlap
with the last tiles on the board!
Think carefully which is the word
that would render you greatest
score. Is it a shoreter word that
when placed forms other words
with already placed letters or a
longer which does not overlap
with any other?

3. Do not rely on the trade function
so much. Nothing guarantees you
will get the letter you want, even if
you choose to trade 3 letters!
Use it only when you cannot
place a word at all!
